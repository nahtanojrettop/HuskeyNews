Talent Show

As we all know the talent show is coming soon.
The original plan for the talent show was to put up three sign up sheets in strategic places with ordered colums and rows. But that was not the students plan. The student councels plan was originally to have only a couple of students per grade sign up, but nearly all of the students 1st-5th signed up. The amount of people that signed up simply amazed me. It was complete chaos. Once the rows and colums filled. The students started creating their own rows and colums, until the paper was completely full. Then to add to the mess, they added their own sheets! with absolutely no colums or rows. Miss Louis(the 6th grade aid) said, "The papers all look like graffiti!" and that is exactly what it looks like. Now some of the younger grades are going to be forced to make a hard decision. They must cut most of the students to make room for the actual show I feel bad for the many students that will get cut, but if that is the way it has to be, than it will. I am lucky that I am in the 6th grade because we only had a couple of people sign up, and I was not one of them. Hopefully the talent show will continue talented people.
-SNF
